Creamy Filipino Spaghetti

Satisfy your cravings with this comforting and creamy Cheesy Pinoy Spaghetti! Every bite is packed with flavor, thanks to the tender spaghetti noodles coated in a rich sauce made with sweet banana ketchup and Pinoy-style spaghetti sauce. The ground beef and sliced hotdogs add a savory touch, while the sautéed garlic and onions bring out that homey, familiar taste. Melted butter and a splash of cooking oil make the dish even more indulgent, but the star of the show is the generous topping of grated Eden cheese, making it irresistibly creamy. It's the perfect balance of sweet and savory—a true Filipino family favorite!

PREPARATION

Difficulty

Medium

Serves

2-4

Time

25 mins

INSTRUCTIONS

Try this Filipino staple Cheesy Pinoy Spaghetti with Eden. Savor the creamy, cheesy goodness that makes it flavorful and comforting!!

Ingredients :

Eden Original Cheese, grated 1 cup

Spaghetti Noodles 1 kg

Banana Ketchup 1 Bottle

Pinoy-style spaghetti sauce 560g

Garlic, minced 1 tsp

Ground Beef 3/4 kg

Hotdogs, sliced 5 pcs

Bouillon beef cubes, dissolved in water 2 pcs

Medium onion, chopped 1 pc

Butter 7 tbsp

Salt 2 tbsp

Cooking oil 4 tbsp

Steps in Making :

Cook pasta according to instructions on the packaging. Drain and set aside.

Saute garlic & onion in butter until onions turn translucent.

Add ground beef and cook for 5 mins. Add hotdog and cook for 2 mins. Add the sauce, cubes, ketchup, and water. Simmer for 20 mins. Stir occasionally.

Add Eden Cheese to the sauce to make it creamy. Stir occasionally.

Mix some spaghetti sauce with the pasta and combine to make the pasta red.

Pour remaining spaghetti sauce over pasta. Top with grated Eden Cheese and serve!
