Pork Giniling

Pork Giniling is a simple yet comforting dish that's perfect for any family meal. Ground pork is slowly simmered in a rich tomato sauce, absorbing all the flavors while diced potatoes and carrots add a hearty touch. Garlic, onions, and a hint of sugar give it a nice balance of savory and sweet. For an extra treat, you can add boiled eggs to make it even more filling. Served over rice, this dish is a delicious go-to for busy weeknights or weekend gatherings!

PREPARATION

Difficulty

Medium

Serves

6

Time

40 mins

INSTRUCTIONS

A pork dish that makes use of ground pork as the primary ingredient. Ground pork is stewed in tomato sauce and water to bring out the taste while vegetables such as carrots and potatoes!!

Ingredients :

Ground Pork 1 1/2 lbs.

Potatoes, diced 1 1/2 cups

Carrots, diced 1 cup

Tomato sauce 8 oz.

Garlic cloves, crushed 6 pcs

Onion, minced 1 pc (medium sized)

Granulated sugar 1 tsp

Beef/Pork cube 1 pc

Boiled eggs, shelled (optional) 4 pcs

Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking oil 3 tbsp

Water 1 cup

Steps in Making :

Heat a cooking pot and pour-in the cooking oil. Then if the oil is hot enough, put-in the garlic and sauté until the color turns light brown.

Add the onions and sauté until the texture becomes soft, then put-in the ground pork afterwards and cook for 5 minutes.

Add the beef or pork cube, tomato sauce, and water and let boil. Simmer for 20 minutes.

Put the carrots and potatoes in then stir until every ingredient is properly distributed. Simmer for 10 to 12 minutes.

Add salt, ground black pepper, and sugar then stir.

Put in the boiled eggs and turn off the heat. Then transfer to a serving bowl and serve.
