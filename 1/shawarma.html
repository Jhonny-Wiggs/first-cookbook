ShaWarma

This Chicken Shawarma is a game-changer, bringing the bold, vibrant flavors of the Middle East right to your table. Juicy chicken thighs are marinated in a mix of garlic, coriander, cumin, cardamom, and cayenne, then grilled to perfection, locking in all those rich spices. Paired with a creamy yogurt sauce made from Greek yogurt, garlic, and lemon, it’s all wrapped up in warm flatbreads with crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, and a bit of red onion. Top it off with some cheese and a splash of hot sauce, and you’ve got a delicious, homemade shawarma that’s sure to satisfy!

PREPARATION

Difficulty

Medium

Serves

4-5

Time

20 mins

INSTRUCTIONS

This Chicken Shawarma recipe is going to knock your socks off! Just a handful of every day spices makes an incredible Chicken Shawarma marinade that infuses the chicken with exotic Middle Eastern flavours.

Ingredients :

Chicken thighs fillets

Marinade :

Garlic clove, 1 pc

Ground coriander 1 tbsp

Ground cumin 1 tbsp

Ground cardamon 1 tbsp

Ground cayenne pepper 1 tsp (reduce to 1/2 tsp to make it not spicy)

Salt 2 tsp

Black Pepper

Lemon juice 2 tbsp

Olive oil 3 tbsp

Yoghurt Sauce :

Greek Yoghurt 1 cup

Garlic clove 1 pc

Cumin 1 tsp

Squeeze of lemon juice

Salt and pepper

To Serve :

Flatbreads 4-5 pcs

Sliced lettuce

Tomato slices

Red onion

Cheese

Hot sauce of choice

Steps in Making :

Combine the marinade ingredients in a large ziplock bag. Add the chicken, seal, the massage from the outside with your hands to make sure each piece is coated. Marinate 24 hours (minimum 3 hours).

Combine the Yogurt Sauce ingredients in a bowl and mix. Cover and put in the fridge until required (it will last for 3 days in the fridge).

Heat a large non-stick skillet with 1 tablespoon over medium high heat, or lightly brush a BBQ hotplate/grills with oil and heat to medium high. (See notes for baking)

Place chicken in the skillet or on the grill and cook the first side for 4 to 5 minutes until nicely charred. Turn and cook the other side for 3 to 4 minutes (the 2nd side takes less time).

Remove chicken from the grill and cover loosely with foil. Set aside to rest for 5 minutes.

To Serve :

Slice chicken and pile onto platter alongside flatbreads, Salad and the Yoghurt Sauce (or dairy free Tahini sauce from this recipe).

To make a wrap, get a piece of flatbread and smear with Yoghurt Sauce. Top with a bit of lettuce and tomato and Chicken Shawarma. Roll up and enjoy!
