Creamy Garlic Chicken Adobo

Treat your family to a delicious twist on a Filipino favorite with this Creamy Garlic Chicken Adobo! Juicy chicken pieces are cooked in a savory mix of soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic, then made extra rich and flavorful with the addition of butter, Eden Melt Sarap, and all-purpose cream. The result is a perfectly balanced dish that's creamy, garlicky, and packed with the classic adobo flavors you love, but with an indulgent twist. This comforting meal is sure to become a new favorite at your dinner table!

PREPARATION

Difficulty

Medium

Serves

2

Time

25 mins

INSTRUCTIONS

Unleash a burst of flavor with this Filipino favourite, Creamy Garlic Chicken Adobo! This is the perfect savory dish to treat the family to a tasty dinner!!

Ingredients :

Eden Melt Sarap, cubed 30g

Chicken pieces 500g

Garlic, chopped 150g

Dark soy sauce 1/4 cup

Vinegar 2 tbsp

Brown sugar 1 tbsp

Salt 1 tsp

Pepper 1 tsp

All-purpose cream 1/2 cup

Butter 3 tbsp

Steps in Making :

Place the butter in a pot over medium heat and sauté garlic until golden in color. Set aside.

Place the chicken and cook for about 10 minutes.

Pour in dark soy sauce and vinegar and let it simmer for about 5 minutes.

Add in cream and continue cooking.

Add Eden Melt Sarap and mix until dissolved.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Transfer to a plate and garnish with the toasted garlic.
