Creamy Carbonara

Satisfy your cravings with this comforting and creamy Cheesy Pinoy Spaghetti! Every bite is packed with flavor, thanks to the tender spaghetti noodles coated in a rich sauce made with sweet banana ketchup and Pinoy-style spaghetti sauce. The ground beef and sliced hotdogs add a savory touch, while the sautéed garlic and onions bring out that homey, familiar taste. Melted butter and a splash of cooking oil make the dish even more indulgent, but the star of the show is the generous topping of grated Eden cheese, making it irresistibly creamy. It's the perfect balance of sweet and savory—a true Filipino family favorite!

PREPARATION

Difficulty

Medium

Serves

10

Time

20 mins

INSTRUCTIONS

Try a new way to make one of your favorite party classics with this Creamy Carbonara recipe, now with Eden Cream Cheese!!

Ingredients :

Eden Cream Cheese, cut into cubes 1 block

Spaghetti pasta, cooked 250g

Cooking oil 2 tbsp

Garlic minced 3 cloves

White onion, chopped 1/3 cup

Green bell pepper, chopped 1/3 cup

Ham, cut into cubes 1 cup

Bacon 11/2 pcs

All-purpose cream 1 cup

Milk 1 cup

Nutmeg (optional) 1/4 tsp

Sugar 1/4 tsp

Salt and ground pepper to taste

Fresh basi, chopped 3 tbsp

Parmesan cheese 3 tbsp

Steps in Making :

To make the Cream Cheese Carbonara Sauce: In a pan drizzle with oil, sauté the bacon and render the fat. Once cooked, remove the bacon and set aside

Using the same pan, sauté garlic and white onion until slightly caramelized.

Add the ham and green bell pepper. Sauté for 3 minutes.

Add the All Purpose Cream, milk, 1 block of Eden Cream Cheese, 3/4 of the crispy bacon and the parmesan cheese.

Season with nutmeg, sugar, salt and pepper. Simmer for 3 minutes.

Mix in the cooked spaghetti pasta and fresh Basil. Mix just until incorporated.

Transfer to a serving plate top with parmesan cheese and the remaining crispy bacon. Serve and enjoy!
